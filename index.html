One Direction

Selling over 50 million records is no joke, One Direction is an English-Irish band based in London composed of Zayn Malik, Niall Horan, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and Liam Payne. This "teen pop" group unfortunately broke up but still continue as friends and award winning individual artists.

Zayn Malik is an English singer and songwriter who was raised in West Yorkshire. After being eliminated as a solo artist in X Factor, Zayn continued as a member of One Direction, a new group. Later in 2015, Zayn Malik announced his depart from this group and continued as an award winning solo artist with a contract with RCA Records. He grew up listening to his dad's songs, writing rap songs in high school, boxing and spending time with his three sisters. Known as the "Bad Boy" of One Direction, Zayn Malik won "Best Dressed British Male" in 2014 and even launched his own shoe line tied with a world-renowned Italian designer.

Niall Horan is an Irish singer and songwriter, after One Direction split, Niall Horan is a solo artist creating hit songs with contracts with Capitol Records. Niall was born and raised in Ireland by his divorced parents who was discovered by his aunt. After she realized how talented Niall was at singing he performed at various events and signed up for X Factor. Compared to Zayn, Niall focuses on the more "folkier" side of pop bringing elements of folk, pop, soft rock and even country music. He is an active part of the soccer community encouraging younger kids to be apart and helping those who can't through charity.

Early History

Competing in X Factor, the British singing competition, judge Simon Cowell decided to group the five together. Almost instantly, this new group gained popularity across the U.K. and eventually won 3rd place. Their final performance was claimed to have been leaked on the Interenet which led to a 2 million dollar contract with Syco Records.

Success and Departures

Starting from humble beginnings, One Direction made their way to the top. Forbes in 2015 ranked them as the fourth highest earning celebrities in the world and as of 2016 they have sold 50 million records worldwide. But suddenly band member Zayn Malik, decided he would leave 1D to become an individual artist, upseting fans around the world. The others decided to stay together for a year until they all decided to go their own ways in 2016 as solo artists.
