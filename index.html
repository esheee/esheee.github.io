One Direction

One Direction, an English-Irish boy band based in London composed of Zayn Malik, Niall Horan, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and Liam Payne have marked the music industry forever. This "teen pop" group unfortunately broke up but still continue as friends and award winning individual artists.

Early History

Competing in X Factor, the British singing competition, judge Simon Cowell decided to group the five together. Almost instantly, this new group gained popularity across the U.K. and eventually won 3rd place. Their final performance was claimed to have been leaked on the Interenet which led to a 2 million dollar contract with Syco Records.

Success and Departures

Starting from humble beginnings, One Direction made their way to the top.Forbes in 2015 ranked them as the fourth highest earning celebrities in the world and as of 2016 they have sold 50 million records worldwide. But suddenly band member Zayn Malik, decided he would leave 1D to become an individual artist, upseting fans around the world. The others decided to stay together for a year until they all decided to go their own ways in 2016 as solo artists.

Zayn Malik (2nd farthest left) . is an English singer and songwriter who was raised in West Yorkshire. After being eliminated as a solo artist in X Factor, Zayn continued as a member of One Direction, a new group. Later in 2015, Zayn Malik announced his depart from this group and continued as an award winning solo artist with a contract with RCA Records. He grew up listening to his dad's songs, writing rap songs in high school, boxing and spending time with his three sisters. Known as the "Bad Boy" of One Direction, Zayn Malik won "Best Dressed British Male" in 2014 and even launched his own shoe line tied with a world-renowned Italian designer.

Niall Horan (2nd farthest right). is an Irish singer and songwriter, after One Direction split, Niall Horan is a solo artist creating hit songs with contracts with Capitol Records. Niall was born and raised in Ireland by his divorced parents who was discovered by his aunt. After she realized how talented Niall was at singing he performed at various events and signed up for X Factor. Compared to Zayn, Niall focuses on the more "folkier" side of pop bringing elements of folk, pop, soft rock and even country music. He is an active part of the soccer community encouraging younger kids to be apart and helping those who can't through charity.

Harry Styles (Center) also auditioned as a solo artist but with the help of Simon Cowell was the lead singer of One Direction. This English songwriter and singer grew up in Chesire with his older sister and after family issues he deicded to work at the young age of 16 in a bakery. His music as a solo artist is mostly based on elements of pop, soft rock, folk, and Britpop. However Style's works exceed far beyond music, he even starred at Gucci photoshoots and released a new clothing line.

Louis Tomlinson (Farthest right) , another English singer and song writer had a different start to his career. He began his art career acting in the drama film, If I had you . After he was elimated, Louis became a part of the boy band, 1D. In 2013, Louis was signed by Doncaster Rovers as a footballer, and in the same year formed his own record. Louis was born in South Yorkshire and faced numerous family issues. At a young age his parents divorced, and later his mom died of cancer. In high school he failed his classes, worked as a waiter and pursued his passion for the arts by appearing in musical productions. He continued his acting career which he claims motivated him to compete in X Factor, starting his music career. Along with other band members, Louis was extremely involved in charity work and even played for the Soccer Aid 2016. As a solo artist he has made multiple top hits with features and has recently became a father.

Liam Payne (Farthest left) was born and raised in West Midlands, England and also made his debut as a member of One Direction. Early on, he faced several health issues with weak kidneys and had tests done to him every day. Despite this, Liam was a cross country star and he attempted to join the Olympics. In fact, after being eliminated by the judges of X Factor and brought back as a boy band, he eventually dated a judge on X Factor, Cheryl Cole and they now have a son, Bear Payne.Unfortunately, they announced their split in July 2018 but they disclosed they will continue as friends while parenting, Bear. As a solo artist, Liam explores pop with electronic and R&B elements.

Album Cover Description
Up All Night After winning 3rd place on X Factor, the 5 boys with the help of Syco Records released their first debut album, Up All Night. Up All Night is mainly pop which incorporates teen pop, pop rock with electropop influences and was released in November 2011 initially only in the UK and the U.S. but in 2012 spread across the world.
Take Me Home Take Me Home was the second album by One Direction released in November, 2012 which was performed in Madison Square Garden. Globally, this album hit the top charts across over 30 countries with elements of electric guitar, hand claps, and voice harmonies. The album's success marked the first time a group achieved first on the top 200 Nillboard since Danity Kane entered with Welcome to the Doll House.
Midnight Memories Midnight memories is the third album produced by One Direction released in November 2013. This album debuted at number one on the Top 200 Billboards, making it the first group ever to make number 1 for all there of their albums. Compared to the other albums, Midnight memories recieved more mixed critics and reviews due to its "immature" nature.
Four Reflecting its name, Four is the 4th album produced by One Direction released in 2014. This album with positive reviews from critics debuted number one in around 18 different countries. In fact, Four was the 6th best selling album in 2014 with over 3.2 million copies sold around the world. Unfortunately, this was the last album the group produced with band member, Zayn Malik.
Made in the AM This is the 5th album one direction released, however the first without Zayn Malik. Released in 2015 after Zayn's depart, and debuted number 1 in the UK and number 2 in the United States which sold over 2 million copies worldwide.
